Problem Statement

Overview

The goal of this hackathon is to develop a model that accurately forecasts demand for bus journeys at the route level, 15 days prior to the journey date (doj). Predictions will be evaluated using SMAPE (Symmetric Mean Absolute Percentage Error); lower values are better.

Key Factors Influencing Demand

  • Holiday calendars, wedding seasons, long weekends, school vacations, and exam schedules.
  • Regional holidays may affect areas differently, and day-of-week effects shape booking patterns.
  • Not all holidays significantly impact demand; the effect is complex and non-trivial to model.

The Hackathon Challenge

Using the historical booking and search data provided, predict the total number of seats booked for each route exactly 15 days before the date of journey. For example, for a route with doj = 30-Jan-2025, predict the final seat count on 16-Jan-2025 (15 days prior).

Allowed & Disallowed External Data

  • Not allowed: Any similar dataset from public repositories or other aggregators that contain seat booking information.
  • Allowed: Other external data such as public holiday calendars, events, and regional holiday information may be used.

Modeling Tools & Rules

  • Language: Python only.
  • Open-source libraries and frameworks are permitted.

Ethics & Compliance

  • Plagiarism or unethical behaviour will lead to disqualification.
  • Fair play is expected throughout the event.
